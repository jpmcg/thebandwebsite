The BAND Festival

23-25 June 2017 - Dunfanaghy, County Donegal

Welcome to the Bluegrass And Nashville Dunfanaghy Festival

We are assembling a top line-up of Bluegrass musicians, as well as multi-talented Nashville inspired bands. The festival will be taking place in the charming village of Dunfanaghy, surrounded by the awe-inspiring scenery of Donegal.

Confirmed Artists

The Niall Toner Band

The Niall Toner Band have recorded and released six all-original CDs, and signed a US record deal with Pinecastle Records in 2012. They have toured the USA, Germany, Holland and the UK, appearing at many prestigous Festivals, including Merlefest in NC

Two Time Polka

Hailing from County Cork the Two time Polka are an Irish Cajun band and a highlight of any festival they play! With a distinctive sound that has its roots in Cajun, Zydeco, and Bluegrass music they produce a spectacularly vibrant show!

Shawn Jones

Shawn Jones is a regular visitor to Dunfanaghy, where he is appreciated as a singer/songwriter and guitar supremo, but also Shawn spent many years living and playing in Nashville. He regularly tours as lead guitarist for Nashville country star, and multi-Grammy winner, Deanna Carter, as well as playing lead guitar on her latest CD "Southern Way of Life".

Down and Out Bluegrass Band

With members hailing from counties Armagh, Antrim, Down and Dublin, the band play traditional bluegrass straight out of the foothills of Tennessee and North Carolina.

Erdini

Formed by a group of South Donegal musicians Erdini are influenced by both their locality and the extensive culture of Ireland. The songs incorporate elements of bluegrass, country, blues, jazz and traditional Irish music. They are well-known for their often raucous performances, blistering solos and scintillating vocal harmonies!

Woodbine

Regulars on the Irish Festival scene Woodbine are an Irish bluegrass band with their own distinctive sound. With three lead vocals, powerful harmony and exciting instrumentals they play a riveting mix of traditional & contemporary Bluegrass!

Dunfanaghy

Some of Ireland's most beautiful beaches, cliffs, headlands, forest parks and scenry can all be found in the area around Dunfanaghy. The location of Dunfanaghy makes it the perfect base for visiting the beautiful north west.

The village overlooks the magnificant Horn Head, is adjacent to the blue flag Kilahoey beach with its golden sands and crystal clear water, and is a short drive to the picturesque harbour at Portnablagh, the blue flag beach at Marble Hill, Ards Forest Park, Ards Friary, Doe Castle, the Rosguill Peninsula, the Fanad Peninsula, Glenveigh National Park.

Dunfanagy is also located right on the Wild Atlantic Way! Perfect!
